About Us

IndusRoboTix was founded in 2026 by Furqan Khatti in Pakistan. We specialize in assembling custom robotic kits using locally sourced components and our unique custom-designed acrylic chassis.

Our Story

Starting from a passion for robotics and STEM education, we aim to make robotics accessible to students, hobbyists, and professionals across Pakistan.

Owner Bio

Furqan Khatti, a robotics enthusiast with years of experience in mechanical design, leads the team.

Mission

To promote robotics learning and innovation using reliable, locally-made products.

Values
