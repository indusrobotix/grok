About Us

IndusRoboTix, owned by Furqan Khatti, started in 2026 and operates in Pakistan. We buy robotic components from the local market and combine them into custom-built kits with our own designed acrylic chassis. Our focus is on affordable, innovative tech solutions for hobbyists and professionals.
